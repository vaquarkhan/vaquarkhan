### 1. Executive Summary
The Credit Card Analytics Dashboard initiative aims to provide our banking customers with a modern, self-service platform to monitor and analyze their credit card usage patterns. This solution will reduce customer service calls by 40% and enhance customer engagement through interactive visualization of spending patterns.

### 2. Business Context

#### 2.1 Current Situation
- Customers currently need to call customer service for detailed transaction analysis
- No self-service platform for credit card analytics
- Limited visibility into spending patterns
- High volume of customer service requests for basic information

#### 2.2 Business Opportunity
- 65% of customers prefer digital self-service options
- Potential to reduce operational costs by $1.2M annually
- Improve customer satisfaction scores by 25%
- Increase digital engagement by 40%

#### 2.3 Target Users
- Credit card holders
- Small business owners
- Corporate card managers
- Banking relationship managers

### 3. Solution Overview

#### 3.1 Application Components

**Login Portal**
- Secure authentication system
- Password recovery process
- Multi-browser support
- Mobile responsive design

**Analytics Dashboard**
- Credit card summary
- Spending patterns visualization
- Category-wise expenditure
- Monthly trends
- Alert notifications

**Detailed Analysis View**
- Transaction level details
- Custom date range selection
- Export capabilities
- Advanced filtering options

### 4. Business Requirements

#### 4.1 Functional Requirements

**Authentication & Security**
- Secure login mechanism
- Session management
- Data encryption
- Activity logging

**Dashboard Features**
- Real-time credit card balance
- Transaction history
- Spending categorization
- Merchant analysis

**Reporting Capabilities**
- PDF export
- CSV download
- Custom date ranges
- Filtering options

#### 4.2 Non-Functional Requirements

- 99.9% system availability

- Mobile device compatibility
- ADA compliance
